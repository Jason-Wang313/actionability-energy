# Color Palette

Primary colors:

- Ink: `#111827`
- Dark blue: `#0b4f8a`
- Mid blue: `#3f94c5`
- Light blue: `#9ecae1`
- Pale blue: `#d8ecf6`

Support colors:

- Soft gray text: `#4b5563`
- Light gray grid: `#edf0f2`
- Medium gray: `#b8c0cc`
- Orange hard-case accent: `#f3a34d`
- Rose failure accent: `#c94f5c`
- Warm residual map: truncated `magma`, skipping the pure-black low end

Usage:

- Proposed actionability mechanism: dark or mid blue.
- Learned approximations: mid or light blue.
- Negative controls and passive proxies: gray.
- Contact/hard-case caveats: orange.
- Non-executable before-repair futures: rose.
- Residual/energy fields: warm magma-style map, because these figures are energy landscapes rather than score charts.
